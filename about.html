Hello! I'm Alex Rudin, a Masters of Engineering student at UVA in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering with a concentration in Cyber-Physical Systems. I'm passionate about engineering functional, elegant, and empowering solutions to critical problems. Understanding what makes products and processes effective and efficient drives me to continue learning and exploring.

I have four years of industry experience exploring product design and development, construction process development, digital signal processing, embedded hardware for metal 3D printing applications, and hardware and system integration for autonomous vehicle systems.

I'm currently seeking my next opportunity, starting after my graduation in May 2021, developing the next wave of critical automated and robotic systems by utilizing my broad multidisciplinary background and interests, and my experience working in and managing a variety of project environments.
